Problem statement: Full Washers and Dryers

[Washers and dryers not being emptied out in a timely manner after each use, disrupting everyone else trying to do their laundry.]

Affinity diagram: [Issues and Solutions to Washers and Dryers being Abandoned]

The affinity diagram shows what we as a group care about the most when resolving the issue of washers and dryers being left full of laundry. Each category has its respective sticky notes (color-coded) listed beneath. The cause category relates to the causes of people abandoning their clothes, which we determined was mostly because of the app being incorrect at times. Solutions for this can be found in the category titled "Accuracy of Detecting an Open Machine." The other categories include our solutions and other issues. In our solution, we felt a passion for ensuring the security of each person's clothes, as they can be valuable. Our ideas for implementing security in the new system is listed beneath the "Securing Laundry to Each Person" category. Finally, the technology and miscellaneous categories both include ways in which we can upgrade the technology of the dryers to implement our solutions in an efficient manner. We placed especially high priority on cost efficiency.
